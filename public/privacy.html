Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What data we collect

AIFC Transport Management collects and stores:

  • Your email address (for authentication)
  • Business data you enter: consignment notes, lorry receipts, ledger entries
  • App settings and configuration stored in your browser's localStorage

2. How data is stored

Your data is stored in Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database service. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

3. How data is used

Your data is used solely to operate the transport management features. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

4. Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete all data at any time from Settings → Danger Zone → Clear All Data.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the administrator of this application.
